# Worms Sprite Mapping

## Worms Animations (`public/sprites/Worms/`)
- `wbrth.png`: Idle breathing animation (стоять и дышать).
- `wwalk.png`: Walking animation (ходьба).
- `wjump.png`: Forward jump (прыжок вперед).
- `wkamjmp.png`: Backflip / Kamikaze jump (сальто назад).
- `wland1.png`, `wland2.png`: Landing animations (приземление).
- `wdie.png` (or similar grave sprites in Misc): Death / Grave.

### Weapons (Aiming and Holding)
- `wbaz.png`: Bazooka aiming (32 frames, from -90 up to +90 down).
- `wminlnk.png` / `wmini.png`: Minigun holding/aiming.
- `wshotg.png`: Shotgun holding/aiming (used for Triple-Barrel).
- `wmollnk.png` / `wthrow.png`: Throwing or holding items like rockets/grenades.
- `whandf.png` / `whandg.png`: Handgun aiming.
- `wuzi.png`: Uzi aiming.

## Weapon Icons (`public/sprites/Weapon Icons/`)
*Used for the weapon selection menu and UI. `.1` is usually the unselected state, `.2` is the selected state.*
- `bazooka.1.png` / `bazooka.2.png`: Bazooka icon.
- `minigun.1.png` / `minigun.2.png`: Minigun icon.
- `shotgun.1.png` / `shotgun.2.png`: Shotgun (Triple-Barrel) icon.
- `hmissile.1.png` / `hmissile.2.png`: Homing Missile (Rocket Launcher) icon.
- `grenade.1.png`, `dynamite.1.png`, `sheep.1.png`, etc.

## Projectiles & Weapons (`public/sprites/Weapons/`)
- `missile.png`: Standard bazooka rocket.
- `hmissil1.png`: Homing missile.
- `bullet.png`: Minigun/shotgun bullet.
- `grenade.png`: Grenade.

## Misc & Effects
- `public/sprites/Misc/grave1.png` ... `grave6.png`: Graves for dead worms.
- `public/sprites/Misc/cursor*.png`: Crosshairs and cursors.
- `public/sprites/Effects/exbiff.png`, `exfoom.png`: Explosions.
